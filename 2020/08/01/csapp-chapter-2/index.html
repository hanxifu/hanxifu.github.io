Chapter 2

Concepts

  1. unsigned number (无符号整数)

\[ B2U_w(\vec{x}) = \sum_{i=0}^{w-1}x_i2^i \]

  1. two's complement (补码)

\[ B2T_w(\vec{x}) = -x_{w-1}2^{w-1} + \sum_{i=0}^{w-2}x_i2^i \]

Transform

  1. two's complement to unsigned

\[ T2U_w(x) = \begin{cases} x + 2^w, & x < 0 \\ x, & x \geqslant 0 \end{cases} \]

  1. unsigned to two's complement

\[ U2T_w(u) = \begin{cases} u, & u \leqslant TMax_w \\ u - 2^w, & x > Tmax_w \end{cases} \]

Manipulation

  1. unsigned

\[ x *^u_w y = (x * y) \bmod 2^w \]

  1. two's complement

\[ x *^t_w y = U2T_w((x * y) \bmod 2^w) \]
